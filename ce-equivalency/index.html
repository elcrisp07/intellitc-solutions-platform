Methodology

How CE Hour Estimates Are Calculated

Estimation Approach

CE hour equivalencies are estimated based on the time required to complete all learning paths, scenario lab exercises, and assessments within each ICREA certificate tier. These estimates factor in study time, hands-on calculator practice, and assessment preparation. Users should verify CE credit acceptance with their affiliated organization to ensure compliance with their continuing education requirements.

What's Included

Everything your program needs, nothing it doesn't

Every component of IntelliTC is purpose-built for real-world real estate analysis — and purpose-designed for teaching it.

54 Professional Calculators

Investment analysis, cash flow, BRRRR, creative financing, tax strategy, market valuation, and more. Every calculator built to professional standards with real-world inputs and outputs.

60 Interactive Mind Maps

Visual knowledge maps with 1,604 definitions that students can explore at their own pace. Complex concepts broken down into navigable branches — ideal for curriculum supplement or self-study.

Learn Mode

Every calculator includes field-by-field educational content. Students learn why each input matters, not just what to enter. Context, formulas, and industry rationale — built right in.

Consider This Alerts

Contextual risk and opportunity alerts that teach students to think critically about their analysis. Real-world flags surface automatically — turning every calculation into a critical-thinking exercise.

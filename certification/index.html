IntelliTC Real Estate Analyst Certificate

Validate your real estate analysis skills through a structured, three-tier certificate program. Complete learning paths, master scenario labs, and pass comprehensive assessments to earn your ICREA certificate.

Tier 1 — ACREA

ICREA Associate

Demonstrate foundational real estate analysis competency. Ideal for first-time buyers, new investors, and agents building core skills.

📅 25 Questions ⏱ 35 Minutes 🎯 70% to Pass 📚 Pool of 40
Format: Multiple Choice, Scenarios, Calculator Analysis, Workflow Sequencing

Tier 2 — PCREA

ICREA Professional

Prove advanced analytical proficiency across all calculator categories. For experienced investors, agents, and lending professionals.

  • ACREA Certificate + Going Deeper Guide + Investment Strategy Playbook + Professional Services Framework
  • 4 Workflows + 6 Scenario Lab Challenges
  • Pass the Professional Assessment (80% or higher)

Tier 3 — MCREA

ICREA Master

ICREA Certification

IntelliTC Certified Real Estate Analyst

Validate your real estate analysis skills through a structured, three-tier credential program. Complete learning paths, master scenario labs, and pass comprehensive assessments to earn your ICREA designation.

🏅
Tier 1

ICREA Associate

Demonstrate foundational real estate analysis competency. Ideal for first-time buyers, new investors, and agents building core skills.

  • Complete 3 Learning Paths (any combination)
  • Complete 4 Scenario Lab challenges
  • Pass the Associate Assessment (70% or higher)
🏆
Tier 2

ICREA Professional

Prove advanced analytical proficiency across all calculator categories. For experienced investors, agents, and lending professionals.

  • Complete all 6 Learning Paths
  • Complete 8 Scenario Lab challenges (including 2 timed)
  • 10 shared deal analyses on the platform
  • Pass the Professional Assessment (80% or higher)
🌟
Tier 3

ICREA Master

The highest ICREA credential. Demonstrates expert-level mastery of real estate financial analysis, creative structuring, and portfolio strategy.

  • All Professional tier requirements
  • Complete all 12 Scenario Lab challenges
  • Top 25% on timed challenge leaderboard
  • Peer review contributions (5+ reviewed analyses)
  • Pass the Master Assessment (85% or higher)

How ICREA Certification Works

1

Complete Learning Paths

Work through guided learning paths that teach real estate analysis concepts using IntelliTC's 54 calculators.

2

Practice in Scenario Lab

Apply your knowledge to 12 realistic deal scenarios. Analyze properties, answer questions, and compare against expert benchmarks.

3

Pass the Assessment

Take a comprehensive, timed assessment covering all calculator categories. Each tier has its own passing threshold.

4

Earn Your Credential

Receive a verifiable ICREA certificate with your name. Share on LinkedIn, include on your resume, or display in your office.

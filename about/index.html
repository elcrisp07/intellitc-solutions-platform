About

One platform. Every age group. Every experience level.

IntelliTC Solutions is a real estate education ecosystem — 54 professional calculators, six guided learning paths, eighty‑five scenario labs, and a three‑tier certification program — built to help anyone, from a first‑time buyer to a seasoned investor, make smarter real estate decisions.

Our mission

Real estate is the largest financial decision most people will ever make, and most of those decisions are still made on gut instinct and back‑of‑the‑napkin math. Our mission is to put professional‑grade analysis tools — and the education to use them well — into the hands of anyone who needs them, regardless of age, role, or starting experience.

That means a first‑time buyer figuring out what they can really afford. A retiree weighing whether to downsize or stay put. A new agent trying to look like a pro on their next listing presentation. A seasoned investor stress‑testing a rental against rate shocks. The same platform serves all of them, because the math underneath is the same — only the framing changes.

Our vision

We see a generation of real estate decision‑makers — buyers, sellers, agents, investors, lenders, and educators — who never have to choose between a slick‑looking calculator that hides its assumptions and a dense spreadsheet that nobody can read. IntelliTC Solutions is building the connective tissue between practical tools and structured learning, with credentialing built in for those who want to prove they have the chops.

What the platform stands for

Transparent math

Every calculator shows the assumptions behind the numbers — no hidden fees, no rounded‑off inputs, no marketing pretending to be analysis.

Layered for experience

Beginners get plain‑language guidance and Learn Mode. Advanced users get sensitivity analysis, hedge testing, and exportable scenarios. One platform, two reading levels.

Credentials that mean something

The ICREA program (IntelliTC Real Estate Analyst) credentials competence, not seat time. Three tiers — Associate, Professional, Master — each verified through the platform's labs.

Built for every age group

Senior's Corner. Next Gen Corner. Zero‑Experience track. The platform meets users where they are — high‑schoolers and retirees included.

What's inside the platform

IntelliTC Solutions is one connected ecosystem, not a bag of separate tools. The pieces are designed to hand off to each other — a calculator drops you into a scenario lab, the scenario lab drops you into a learning path, the learning path drops you into the certification program.

  • 54 professional calculatorsCash flow, BRRRR, rent vs. buy, 1031 exchange, capital gains, creative finance, DSCR, sensitivity, and more.
  • 6 guided learning pathsFrom buying your first home to mastering BRRRR investing — built around hands‑on calculator exercises.
  • 85 scenario labsGuided challenges, timed drills, and open sandboxes where you analyze, answer, and check your work.
  • ICREA certificationThree tiers — Associate, Professional, Master — verified through the platform's own scenario labs.
  • Senior's Corner & Next Gen CornerDedicated tracks for retirees and for high‑school / early‑career learners.
  • Goal Pilot wizardTell it what you're trying to do; it builds the tool chain for you.

How we operate

IntelliTC Solutions is operated by Infinite Workz Ventures LLC, a Virginia limited liability company headquartered in Virginia Beach, VA, and was founded by Eric L. Crisp. The platform is built and maintained directly — no white‑labeled affiliate stack, no rented calculators, no scraped third‑party content. Every tool, scenario, and learning module on the site was written and tested in‑house.

The educational platform is independent — it does not sell, recommend, or earn a commission on any insurance or investment product. For long‑term capital structure, the parent company works with specialists in policy‑driven financing to keep its balance sheet resilient through cycles, but those arrangements are kept entirely separate from what users see on the site.

Why this matters

Most personal financial mistakes are quiet ones. A mortgage someone shouldn't have signed. A 1031 exchange that didn't actually defer the gain. A rental that cash‑flows on paper and bleeds in real life. A retiree downsizing into a higher monthly payment than they thought.

None of those decisions get fixed by another flashy tool. They get fixed by a person, in front of an honest calculator, with a clear explanation of what the numbers mean. That's what we build — for first‑time buyers, agents, investors, lenders, students, retirees, and everyone in between.

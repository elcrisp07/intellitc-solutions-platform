1

Create a Google Analytics Account

Go to analytics.google.com and sign in with your Google account.

Click "Start measuring" and follow the prompts:

  • Account name: IntelliTC Solutions
  • Property name: IntelliTC Platform
  • Time zone: your time zone
  • Currency: USD
  • Business info: select what fits

When asked about a data stream, choose "Web" and enter your website URL.

2

Copy Your Measurement ID

After creating the property, Google will show you a Measurement ID. It looks like this:

Copy that ID. It's also found under Admin → Data Streams → your stream → Measurement ID.

What is Cost Segregation?
Cost segregation reclassifies building components from the standard 27.5-year (residential) or 39-year (commercial) depreciation schedule into shorter 5, 7, and 15-year recovery periods. This accelerates your depreciation deductions, creating immediate tax savings and improving cash flow. Components such as carpet, appliances, landscaping, and certain electrical systems can qualify for accelerated treatment — often yielding substantial first-year tax benefits when combined with bonus depreciation.
